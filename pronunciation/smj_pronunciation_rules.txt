* Epenthetic vowels in third grade (Q3), usually between sonorants /r,l,j,v/ and heterorganic consonants, the quality of which is usually identical to that of the preceding vowel.
* 1st syllable diphthongs are shorter before Q3 consonant than before Q2 consonant.
* (plain text does not support small superscript letters)
